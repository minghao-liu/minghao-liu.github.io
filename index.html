Short Bio

I am currently a PhD student in Computer Software and Theory at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and doing my research at State Key Laboratory of Computer Science, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Zhang. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Northeast Normal University (NENU) in 2017.

My research interests include the theory, algorithm and application of automated reasoning, constraint programming and combinatorial optimization, as well as the integration of symbolic reasoning and machine learning.
